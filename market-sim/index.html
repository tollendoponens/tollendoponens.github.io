Curve shape

Externalities

Charged to every student except the two in the trade, as real money off their round. Negative is an external cost (pollution), positive a benefit. Quarters allowed, and you'll want them: at 20 students, $1 each is $18 a unit socially, which swamps every trade. Students are told about it — the point is that knowing changes nothing, since the traders themselves never pay.

Structure and shape take effect at the next deal.
